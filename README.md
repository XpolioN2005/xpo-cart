# Sketch Racers

A fast-paced 2D multiplayer physics racing game where players design and build their own flying vehicles before every race.

Instead of choosing from preset cars, every player creates a unique machine by drawing its body and placing functional components such as thrusters. Your engineering decisions directly affect how the vehicle accelerates, flies, lands, and handles across dynamic procedural tracks.

Built with **Godot 4**.

---

## Features

### Vehicle Builder

Create a completely custom vehicle before each race.

- Draw your own vehicle body
- Place and reposition thrusters
- Customize colors
- Save and load builds
- Every design changes gameplay

Examples:

- Rear thrusters provide faster acceleration.
- Bottom thrusters improve lift and recovery.
- Larger bodies gain momentum but require additional thrust.

There are no predefined vehicles. Every racer is player-made.

---

### Physics-Based Racing

Easy to learn, difficult to master.

Controls include:

- Forward
- Reverse
- Fly (Thruster)

Gravity is always active, making vehicle balance and control an important part of every race.

---

### Procedural Tracks

Race across procedurally generated environments featuring:

- Roads
- Cliffs
- Caves
- Floating islands
- Aerial shortcuts
- Environmental hazards

Every race offers a different layout.

---

### Power-Ups

Collect random pickups during races to gain temporary advantages.

Planned power-ups include:

- Rocket Boost
- Shield
- Oil Spill
- More to come...

Power-ups are designed to enhance gameplay without replacing player skill.

## Visual Style

Sketch Racers uses a minimalist hand-drawn art style.

- White paper background
- Black outlines
- Simple geometric shapes
- Scribbled particle effects
- Hand-drawn animations
- Bright accent colors for players and power-ups

The goal is readability, charm, and personality rather than realism.

---

## Gameplay Loop

1. Build or load a vehicle
2. Join a race
3. Navigate the procedural course
4. Collect power-ups
5. Outsmart your opponents
6. Win the race
7. Improve your design and race again
